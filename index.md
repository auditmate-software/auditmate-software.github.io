# SOC 2 Evidence Collector (Linux)

*Audit‑Mate — Your Linux SOC 2 Type 1 evidence automation tool.*

---

A lightweight, self-hosted tool that automatically collects SOC 2 Type 1 evidence from Linux systems and packages it into auditor-ready artifacts. Designed for small teams and startups preparing for their first SOC 2 audit without paying for expensive SaaS platforms.

## Features

- Collects system, user, SSH, firewall, package, service, and log evidence
- Generates auditor-ready ZIP files for SOC 2 Type 1
- Self-hosted, no cloud SaaS required
- Simple command-line usage for fast setup

## Who is it for

- Small teams and startups getting ready for their first SOC 2 audit
- Companies looking to reduce audit prep costs
- Technical teams who want full control over evidence collection
